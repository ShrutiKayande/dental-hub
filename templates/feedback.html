Frequently Asked Questions

How long do dental implants last? +
Dental implants can last 20+ years or even a lifetime with proper care. Regular dental checkups, good oral hygiene, and avoiding hard foods help maintain them.
Is root canal treatment painful? +
No, modern root canal treatments are virtually painless. We use advanced local anesthesia and rotary endodontics to ensure your complete comfort throughout the procedure.
Do you offer flexible payment options? +
Yes, we offer EMI options and accept all major insurance plans. We also accept credit/debit cards, UPI, and cash payments. Contact us for payment plans.
How often should I visit the dentist? +
We recommend visiting the dentist every 6 months for routine checkups and professional cleaning. Regular visits help prevent dental problems and maintain oral health.
What types of dental treatments do you offer? +
We offer complete dental care including implants, RCT, braces & aligners, crowns & bridges, teeth whitening, extractions, pediatric dentistry, and smile makeovers.
Do you treat children? +
Yes, we specialize in pediatric dentistry. Our child-friendly environment and gentle approach make dental visits comfortable and positive for children.
What is the cost of common dental procedures? +
Costs vary depending on the procedure and complexity. We provide transparent estimates after consultation. Please call us for approximate pricing.
Do you accept dental insurance? +
Yes, we accept most major dental insurance plans. Our staff can help you verify coverage and submit claims on your behalf.
How do I maintain my braces or aligners? +
Brush twice daily, avoid hard/sticky foods, and attend regular follow-ups. We provide complete guidance and cleaning kits for braces and aligners.
Is teeth whitening safe? +
Yes, professional teeth whitening under dental supervision is completely safe. We use enamel-safe whitening agents for optimal results without sensitivity.
